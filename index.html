How the game works

Help your fellow teammates defeating the Pokémon League before the other platforms and enter the Hall of Fame.

Pick a Pokémon game or a streaming platform of your choice and join hundreds of players form all around the world. Control your trainer by sending the well-known GameBoy commands A, B, UP, DOWN, RIGHT, LEFT, START, SELECT from any of the Platform's chat. Each platform is bound to one of the games.

YouTube

The platform was not born for streaming (and it shows); also the development of the bot that controls the game has been cumbersome compared to the others and is not suitable for such a thing ... But he doesn't know it, and it still works... [semi cit]. Through YouTube you can play Pokémon Red.

Mixer

Born as a response to Twitch's monopoly, despite its attractive graphics and tools that make the streaming experience even more pleasant than its competitors, it never emerged quite as much. It was paired with Pokémon Blue, because of the colour similarity and because of the resemblance in being the "eternal second" just like the platform that hosts it.

Twitch

Considered as the favored to win, with a wider and more prepared audience, Twitch is the last (but not least) platform. Pokémon Yellow (perhaps because there is no purple Pokémon version) is the game controlled by this platform, will it succeed in winning hands down?

Telegram

Telegram is not used to command any game directly, but it's a very powerful and strategic tool, in fact, it is used to take part to raids! When the green bar at the bottom of the streaming is full, every player who subscribed to raids will be asked a question about the game that is being played. The platform that will provide the most correct answers will win the raid and get a bonus or set an handicap their opponents.

Instagram

We are very fond of statistics, analytics and charts, we publish a lot of stuff on our Instagram pages, if you are anything like us, make sure to follow our pages! You could see your name as the player who hit the LEFT command the most between 3 and 4 AM.

What next?

When the first challenge will end, a new exciting one will begin: the second, third, fourth (and more...) generations will be streamed, with many features added each time, stay tuned...
